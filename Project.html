Safe injection sites are locations where people who are addicted to harmful substances can go and have access to clean needles and administration under medical professionals.

What is it?

They allow for the removal of needles deaths from drug overdoses and prevent deadly disease transferring due to the removal of unclean needles. They additionally may have econimocally benifits if they are implemented in heavy drug use areas.
